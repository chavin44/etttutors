About Us

Kevin Bheemarasetti and Nithin Ravikumar are two passionate students who share a deep love for computer science projects and competitive programming. Their dedication and enthusiasm for technology have driven them to excel in their academic pursuits. With a keen interest in the field, they aspire to major in computer science during their college years, further honing their skills and knowledge. Recognizing the importance of sharing their expertise, they founded Emerging Tech Tutoring, an initiative aimed at helping students navigate the ever-evolving world of technology. Through their tutoring services, they strive to inspire and empower others, fostering a love for computer science and paving the way for future generations of tech enthusiasts.
