About AI & ML

Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) are rapidly growing fields that are transforming various industries and shaping the future of technology. These technologies involve developing intelligent systems that can learn from data, recognize patterns, and make decisions or predictions. AI and ML algorithms are used in areas such as computer vision, natural language processing, robotics, and predictive analytics, and are being applied across various industries, including healthcare, finance, and transportation.

You should take this course if you want to:

    ○ Understand the principles and applications of AI and ML, which are driving innovation in areas such as computer vision, natural language processing, and predictive analytics.

    ○ Develop skills in working with large datasets, building and training machine learning models, and interpreting their results.

    ○ Gain exposure to cutting-edge technologies and techniques that are in high demand across various industries.

    ○ Explore the ethical considerations and societal implications of AI and ML, which are becoming increasingly important as these technologies become more prevalent.
