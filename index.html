What We Offer

At Emerging Tech Tutoring, we are dedicated to empowering the next generation with cutting-edge programming skills. Our comprehensive program is designed to provide an immersive learning experience, equipping children with the tools they need to thrive in the digital age.

Extensive Tutoring

We offer an 8-week semester of personalized tutoring, ensuring that each student receives the attention and guidance they deserve. Our instructors will tailor the curriculum to your child's unique learning style and pace.

Comprehensive Resources

In addition to hands-on instruction, we provide comprehensive notes and resources to reinforce the concepts learned during the sessions. These materials serve as valuable references for future learning and exploration.

Live Support

We understand that learning can be a challenging journey, which is why we offer live help whenever needed. Our dedicated community is available to to help with any questions or concerns, ensuring that your child's progress is never hindered.

Certification and Recognition

Upon successful completion of the program, students will have the opportunity to take a certification test, validating their newly acquired skills and knowledge. This certification serves as a testament to their hard work and dedication.

Collaborative Community

Learning is not a solitary endeavor. We foster a vibrant Discord community where students can connect, collaborate, and support one another. This platform encourages peer-to-peer learning, fostering a sense of camaraderie and shared growth.

Highly Qualified Instructors

Our instructors are carefully selected for their expertise, passion, and ability to inspire young minds. They bring a wealth of knowledge and real-world experience to the classroom, ensuring that your child receives top-notch instruction from industry professionals.

At Emerging Tech Tutoring, we believe in empowering the next generation with the skills they need to thrive in the digital age. Join us on this exciting journey and unlock your child's full potential.
